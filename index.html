What is HackEEE

A 24 hour hackathon, from the EEE Vertical. We calls for coders, tech evangelists, creatives, and developers from all disciplines to take a part to win awesome prizes and collaborate with other developers. We are determined to have an all-inclusive and diverse group of students attending.

Themes

Building Blocks


Tackle exciting challenges and design cutting-edge solutions the bare bones way. Delve into advanced topics, from power electronics to integrated circuits, and become an electronics maestro. Turn your ideas into reality through hands-on prototyping and testing of your transistor-powered creations

Smart Campus


Challenge the status quo with your inventive solutions. We invite you to share your game-changing ideas to reimagine campus life. Not just about brainstorming; roll up your sleeves and turn your ideas into practical, impactful projects.
